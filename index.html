Russia-Ukraine War

In the face of the Russia-Ukraine War, the haunting possibility of World War 3 threathened once again. Let's explore the reactions and preparadness of countries around the world following Russia's invasion of Ukraine on the 24th of February 2022.

Catching Up

In an effort to fight against Russia, Ukraine have had to up their military spending, pushing their military spend per GDP to be the highest in the world. No country in the past 10 years has come close to focusing such a high proportion of their GDP on their military.

Not Enough

Despite this increase in focus on military spending, Ukraine's overall spending budget still remained dwarfed by Russia's. Recognising this, allying countries around the world have come to Ukraine's aid in an effort to fight against the invading Russia.
